🤖 AI Interview Scheduling Automation

Built an automated interview scheduling workflow using Make.com, Google Sheets, Google Calendar, Gemini AI and Gmail.

Key Features

  • Candidate data fetched from Google Sheets
  • Google Calendar event created automatically
  • Google Meet link generated
  • AI Interview email generated
  • Google Sheet updated automatically
  • Gmail invitation sent automatically

Automation Result

The workflow automatically schedules interviews, creates Google Meet links, updates candidate records and sends interview invitation emails.

Tools Used: Make.com • Google Sheets • Google Calendar • Gemini AI • Gmail
