🎯 ICP Research & Lead Enrichment

Performed ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) research and lead enrichment using Clay, Apollo.io, LinkedIn Sales Navigator and HubSpot CRM to build high-quality prospect lists for GTM and outbound sales campaigns.

Clay Lead Enrichment Workflow

Business Problem

Sales teams often spend significant time searching for qualified leads and validating company information manually.

Solution

  • Identified Ideal Customer Profiles (ICP)
  • Collected company information
  • Performed lead enrichment using Clay
  • Verified contacts using Apollo.io
  • Updated qualified leads in HubSpot CRM

Business Outcome

  • Higher quality leads
  • Improved sales readiness
  • Reduced manual research effort
  • Organized CRM-ready database

Tools Used

Clay • Apollo.io • LinkedIn Sales Navigator • HubSpot CRM
