🤖 AI Interview Scheduling Automation

This workflow automates interview scheduling by integrating Google Sheets, Google Calendar, Gemini AI and Gmail using Make.com.

Workflow

Business Problem

Scheduling interviews manually takes time and often results in repetitive work. This automation eliminates manual scheduling and communication.

Solution

  • Candidate details fetched from Google Sheets
  • Google Calendar event created automatically
  • Google Meet link generated
  • Gemini AI generated interview email
  • Candidate record updated automatically
  • Gmail invitation sent automatically

Automation Result

Tools Used

Make.com (Basic) • Google Sheets • Google Calendar • Gemini AI • Gmail
